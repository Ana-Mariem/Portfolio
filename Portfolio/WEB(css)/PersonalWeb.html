Background

Since I was little I have always enjoyed drawing, as well as colors and observing my surroundings. Growing up I became very interested in cinema and the endless possibilities it offered to create stories and new worlds. So, digital animation seemed like a great idea to me; combining something I enjoyed with technology. My goal as a future professional is to be able to work in a film studio or in the production of a video game, being able to contribute with my ideas and skills in the creation of something that people can enjoy.
